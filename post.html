10 Tips to Overcome Writer’s Block

Posted on May 8, 2025

Writer's block is a common challenge. Here are 10 practical strategies to help you write again:

  1. Take a short walk to reset your brain.
  2. Free write for 10 minutes without stopping.
  3. Write in a different location or setting.
  4. Read something inspiring in your genre.
  5. Change your writing tool (notebook vs laptop).
  6. Use a writing prompt to kickstart creativity.
  7. Talk through your idea with a friend.
  8. Set a 15-minute timer and just begin.
  9. Skip ahead to a scene you’re excited about.
  10. Allow yourself to write badly — editing comes later.
